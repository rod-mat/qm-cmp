About

Learning and research group on quantum mechanics and condensed matter (and statistical mechanics). The Research Path depends on your level (year of study).

Research Path

  1. Linear Algebra
    1. Linear Vector Spaces
    2. Inner Product Spaces
    3. Dual Spaces
    4. Linear Operators
    5. Matrix Elements of Linear Operators
    6. Generalization to Infinite Dimensions
  2. Basics of Group Theory
  3. Introduction to Quantum Mechanics and Operators
  4. Introduction to Solid State Physics
  5. Electronic Structure
  6. Density Functional Theory (Optional)
  7. Profundization Topics (to choose)
    1. Topological Phases of Matter
    2. Magnetism
